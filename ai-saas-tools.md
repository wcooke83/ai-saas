## Tier 1: High-Demand, Easy to Build (Recommended)

### 1. AI Content Repurposing Tool
**Demand**: Very High | **Difficulty**: Low-Medium | **Profit Potential**: $$$

Convert one piece of content into multiple formats:
- Blog post → 10 social posts + newsletter + video script
- Podcast → Blog + quotes + audiograms
- Video → Shorts + transcript + blog

**Why it sells**: Content marketing is demanding, teams are small. Saves 10+ hours/week. ([Next Rocket](https://www.nextrocket.io/blog/5-micro-saas-ideas-you-can-build-in-2025-with-ai-and-generate-8k-monthly))

**Tech Stack**: OpenAI API + simple UI
**Pricing Model**: $29-79/month or $149-299 lifetime
**Competition**: Medium (Repurpose.io, quso.ai)
