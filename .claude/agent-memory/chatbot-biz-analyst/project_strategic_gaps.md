name: VocUI Strategic Gaps & Recommendations
description: Top strategic priorities identified in the March 2026 business audit
type: project
---

**Central finding:** The product is ahead of the business. Marketing undersells VocUI significantly. Biggest near-term lever is marketing and positioning, not product development.

**Priority 1 (Immediate): Fix the marketing-product gap.**
Homepage hero has probable copy error ("trained on VocUI" instead of "trained on your knowledge base"). Homepage features grid omits calendar booking, agent handoff, sentiment analysis. Pricing page social proof is thin and testimonials appear fabricated (Davidson Marketing Co., TechStart Inc., Nexus Digital — unverifiable generic names).

**Priority 2 (Immediate): Fix credit transparency.**
No persistent credit counter in sidebar. No in-app alerts at 75%/90% usage. Surprise exhaustion drives churn. Add credit indicator and proactive alerts.

**Priority 3 (High): Account-level onboarding.**
Zero account-level onboarding flow. New users land on dashboard showing credits/API keys — meaningless before first chatbot. No celebration of activation milestone. Onboarding checklist state is localStorage-based (not server-synced, breaks across devices).

**Priority 4 (High): Elevate hidden features.**
16 tabs per chatbot (6 primary + 10 in "More" dropdown). Agent Console, Sentiment, Performance, Leads buried. Rename "Agent Console" to "Live Conversations" for non-technical users.

**Priority 5 (Quick win): Deploy VocUI on vocui.com.**
The marketing site does not use its own product for support. This is the strongest product demo possible.

**Priority 6 (Medium): Webhooks / integration layer.**
`webhooks` table exists in DB — suggests planned. Without webhooks or Zapier, buyers who use HubSpot/Salesforce/Slack are blocked. This is a disqualifier for SMB buyers.

**Priority 7 (Medium): Real social proof.**
Get on G2/Capterra, replace fabricated testimonials with verifiable quotes, publish 1-2 case studies with quantified outcomes.

**Priority 8 (Low effort, high impact): Custom branding for all paid plans.**
Remove VocUI branding from widget for Base+ subscribers, not just Enterprise. Use it as Free → paid conversion lever.

**Priority 9 (Longer term): Calendar booking as a vertical play.**
Build a dedicated landing page for service businesses (clinics, law firms, coaches). Calendar booking is a genuine differentiator not visible in marketing.

**Why:** These were conclusions from a full codebase and UX audit in March 2026.

**How to apply:** Use this as a prioritized backlog for strategic conversations. Don't recommend more product features before the marketing and onboarding gaps are closed.
