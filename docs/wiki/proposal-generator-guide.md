# Proposal Generator

Create polished business proposals with customizable sections, industry-specific language, and export to PDF, Word, or Markdown.

## How It Works

1. Configure **proposal settings** — type, industry, and tone
2. Enter **client** and **company** information
3. Describe the **project** with objectives, timeline, and budget
4. Select which **sections** to include
5. Click **Generate** to create your proposal
6. Edit any section, then **export** in your preferred format

## Proposal Types

| Type | Structure |
|------|-----------|
| **Sales** | Value-focused with ROI, benefits, and pricing |
| **RFP Response** | Requirements-aligned with compliance sections |
| **Project** | Deliverables, milestones, and timeline-focused |
| **Service** | Ongoing services with SLAs and support tiers |
| **Technical** | Architecture, specs, and implementation details |
| **Staffing** | Team composition, roles, and resource allocation |
| **Retainer** | Monthly/recurring services with scope definitions |
| **Maintenance** | Support agreements with response times and coverage |

## Sections

Choose which sections to include. Defaults are set based on your proposal type, but you can toggle any on or off:

- Executive Summary
- Problem Statement
- Proposed Solution
- Scope of Work
- Timeline & Milestones
- Team & Qualifications
- Pricing & Investment
- Terms & Conditions
- Case Studies
- Technical Approach
- Risk Assessment

## Tips for Better Proposals

### Project Description

Be thorough — this is the foundation of your proposal. Include:
- What the client needs
- Current challenges or pain points
- Any relevant background or context
- Specific requirements mentioned in the RFP (if applicable)

### Competitive Advantage

This optional field has a big impact. The AI weaves your differentiators throughout the proposal rather than listing them in one section. Include:
- Years of experience or domain expertise
- Past results with similar clients
- Proprietary tools or methodology
- Awards, certifications, or notable clients

### Industry Selection

Choosing the right industry adds relevant terminology, compliance references, and sector-specific best practices to your proposal.

## Editing & Export

After generation, each section is individually editable. You can:
- **Edit** any section's content directly
- **Regenerate** a single section without affecting others
- **Export as PDF** — Professional layout ready to send
- **Export as Word** — Editable .docx for further customization
- **Export as Markdown** — Plain text for version control or documentation
