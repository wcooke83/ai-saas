# Blog Writer

Create complete blog posts in a guided 3-step process: generate an outline, refine it, then produce the full article with SEO optimization.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Blog Post Details

Configure your article:
- **Post Format** — Determines the article structure
- **Tone** — Sets the writing voice
- **Topic** — What the article is about (required, min 10 characters)
- **Target Audience** — Who you're writing for
- **SEO Keywords** — Comma-separated terms for search optimization
- **Word Count** — Short (~500), Medium (~1000), Long (~2000), or Custom

### Step 2: Outline Editor

Review and edit the AI-generated outline:
- Reorder sections by dragging
- Add or remove sections
- Edit section headings
- Approve the outline to proceed

### Step 3: Full Article

The complete blog post is generated based on your approved outline. Review, copy, or export the final article.

## Post Formats

| Format | Structure | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Listicle** | Numbered list of items | "10 Ways to...", "Top 5..." |
| **How-to** | Step-by-step instructions | Tutorials, processes |
| **Tutorial** | Detailed walkthrough | Technical guides |
| **Review** | Evaluation with pros/cons | Product reviews |
| **Case Study** | Problem/solution narrative | Success stories |
| **Opinion** | Argument-based essay | Thought leadership |
| **Comparison** | Side-by-side analysis | "X vs Y" articles |
| **Guide** | Comprehensive resource | Pillar content |

## SEO Keywords

When you provide keywords, the AI:
- Incorporates them naturally into headings and subheadings
- Uses them in the introduction and conclusion
- Distributes them throughout body paragraphs
- Avoids keyword stuffing for natural readability

Separate multiple keywords with commas: `email marketing, open rates, subject lines`

## Word Count Guide

| Preset | Words | Use Case |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Short** | ~500 | Quick updates, news, announcements |
| **Medium** | ~1,000 | Standard blog posts, opinion pieces |
| **Long** | ~2,000 | In-depth guides, pillar content |
| **Custom** | 300-5,000 | Specific requirements |

## Tips for Better Articles

- **Be specific with your topic** — "How SaaS companies can reduce churn with onboarding emails" produces better results than "email marketing tips"
- **Define your audience** — This adjusts vocabulary, examples, and assumed knowledge level
- **Edit the outline** — The outline is your chance to shape the article before it's written. Remove irrelevant sections or add ones the AI missed
- **Use Additional Instructions** — Tell the AI to include specific data, mention your product, or follow a particular style guide
