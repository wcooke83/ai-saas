# Understanding Credits & Usage

Credits are the currency that powers your AI tools and chatbot interactions. This guide explains how they work and how to manage them.

## What Are Credits?

Credits represent your monthly token allowance. Every interaction with an AI tool or chatbot consumes tokens, which count against your credit balance.

**Tokens** are the fundamental unit that AI models use to process text:
- ~4 characters = 1 token (English text)
- A typical sentence = 15-25 tokens
- A paragraph = 50-100 tokens

## What Consumes Credits?

| Action | Approximate Cost |
|--------|-----------------|
| Chatbot message (visitor question + bot response) | 200-800 tokens |
| Email Writer generation | 500-1,500 tokens |
| Blog Post generation | 1,000-3,000 tokens |
| Proposal generation | 2,000-5,000 tokens |
| Social Post generation | 200-500 tokens |
| Ad Copy generation | 300-700 tokens |
| Meeting Notes generation | 500-2,000 tokens |

Actual usage depends on the length of your input and the AI's response.

## Monthly Reset

Credits reset at the beginning of each billing period. Unused credits **do not roll over** to the next month.

Your billing period start date is shown on the **Dashboard** and **Billing** pages.

## Monitoring Usage

### Dashboard

The **Credits Remaining** card on your dashboard shows:
- Credits used vs. total allocation
- A color-coded progress bar (green → yellow → red as usage increases)

### Usage Page

Navigate to **Dashboard > Usage** for detailed tracking:
- Token consumption over time
- Breakdown by tool type
- Individual generation history

## Plan Limits

Each subscription plan includes a monthly credit allocation:

- Exceed your limit and generations will be paused until the next billing period
- Upgrade your plan at any time to get more credits
- Enterprise plans offer custom credit allocations

Check the **Dashboard** overview or **Billing** page to view your current plan and upgrade options.

## Auto Top-Up

If you frequently run out of credits before your billing period resets, you can enable **Auto Top-Up**:

1. Go to **Dashboard > Billing**
2. Find the **Auto Top-Up** section
3. Configure the threshold and top-up amount
4. Credits are automatically added when your balance drops below the threshold

## Tips for Managing Credits

- **Use the right model tier** — "Fast" tier uses fewer tokens than "Powerful" for chatbots
- **Keep prompts concise** — Shorter system prompts and context use fewer tokens
- **Set max tokens** — Configure a maximum response length in chatbot settings
- **Monitor regularly** — Check the Usage page weekly to avoid surprises
- **Optimize knowledge base** — Well-chunked content means the chatbot retrieves less unnecessary context
