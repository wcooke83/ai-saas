# Email Writer

Generate polished, professional emails in seconds. The Email Writer crafts complete emails with subject lines tailored to your purpose, audience, and desired tone.

## How It Works

1. Select an **email type** and **tone**
2. Enter sender and recipient details
3. Describe the **purpose** of your email
4. Optionally add key points and a call to action
5. Click **Generate** to create your email

The AI generates a complete email with a compelling subject line and properly structured body text.

## Email Types

| Type | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **Cold Outreach** | First contact with a potential client or partner |
| **Follow Up** | Continuing a conversation after a meeting or previous email |
| **Introduction** | Introducing yourself or connecting two people |
| **Thank You** | Expressing gratitude after a meeting, purchase, or favor |
| **Meeting Request** | Requesting a call or meeting with clear time/agenda |
| **Proposal** | Pitching a product, service, or partnership |
| **Feedback Request** | Asking for input, reviews, or testimonials |

## Tone Options

- **Formal** — Best for executives, legal contexts, or first contact with enterprise clients
- **Professional** — Standard business communication with a balance of warmth and authority
- **Friendly** — Peer-to-peer or warm lead communication with a conversational feel
- **Casual** — Informal contexts, internal team, or established relationships

## Tips for Better Results

### Be Specific with Purpose

The **Purpose** field is the most important input. Instead of "I want to sell our product," try:

> "I want to introduce our automated reporting tool to marketing directors who currently spend 5+ hours per week on manual reports. Reference their company's recent growth announcement."

### Use Key Points Strategically

List specific details you want included:
- Statistics or results ("saved 40% on reporting time")
- Personal references ("noticed your LinkedIn post about...")
- Specific product features to highlight
- Deadline or urgency factors

### Set a Clear Call to Action

A strong CTA makes follow-through easy:
- "Book a 15-minute demo at [link]"
- "Reply with your availability this week"
- "Review the attached proposal by Friday"

## Output

The generated email includes:
- **Subject Line** — Optimized for open rates based on email type
- **Email Body** — Properly formatted with greeting, body paragraphs, and closing

Use the copy buttons to grab the subject, body, or full email individually. Click the refresh icon to regenerate with the same inputs for a different variation.
