# Email Sequence Builder

Generate complete multi-email sequences with subject lines, body copy, scheduling, and strategic guidance for each email.

## How It Works

1. Select a **sequence type** and **tone**
2. Enter **sender details** (name, role, company)
3. Define your **target audience** and industry
4. Describe the **campaign goal**, product/service, and unique value
5. Optionally add pain points, a CTA, and additional context
6. Choose the **number of emails** (3, 5, or 7)
7. Click **Generate** to create the full sequence

Each email includes a subject line, full body copy, the recommended day to send it, and its strategic purpose in the sequence.

## Sequence Types

| Type | Best For | Default Emails | Day Spacing |
|------|----------|----------------|-------------|
| **Cold Outreach** | Reaching prospects who don't know you yet | 5 | Days 0, 3, 7, 14, 21 |
| **Follow-Up** | After initial contact or a meeting | 3 | Days 0, 2, 5 |
| **Customer Onboarding** | Welcoming and guiding new customers | 5 | Days 0, 1, 3, 7, 14 |
| **Re-Engagement** | Winning back inactive users or customers | 3 | Days 0, 5, 14 |
| **Sales Nurture** | Moving leads through the sales funnel | 7 | Days 0, 3, 7, 10, 14, 21, 28 |
| **Event Promotion** | Webinars, conferences, or product launches | 5 | Countdown to event |
| **Product Launch** | Building anticipation and driving launch sales | 5 | Pre-launch countdown |
| **Feedback Request** | Collecting reviews, testimonials, or NPS scores | 3 | Post-interaction |

## Tone Options

| Tone | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **Formal** | Enterprise outreach, legal, finance |
| **Professional** | Standard B2B communication |
| **Friendly** | Peer-to-peer, warm leads, community |
| **Casual** | Consumer brands, internal comms |
| **Persuasive** | Sales sequences, promotions, event signups |

## Input Fields

### Required

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Sender Name** | Your name as it appears in the "From" line |
| **Target Audience** | Who you're emailing (min 5 chars, e.g., "SaaS founders with 10-50 employees") |
| **Campaign Goal** | What you want to achieve (min 10 chars) |
| **Product or Service** | What you're promoting or discussing (min 3 chars) |
| **Unique Value** | Your differentiator (min 10 chars) |

### Optional

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Sender Role** | Your job title |
| **Sender Company** | Your company name |
| **Target Industry** | The industry you're targeting |
| **Pain Points** | Specific problems your audience faces |
| **Call to Action** | What you want recipients to do |
| **Additional Context** | Any extra details to shape the sequence |

## Output

Each generated sequence includes:

- **Summary** — Overview of the sequence strategy
- **Individual Emails** — Each with:
  - Email number and recommended send day
  - Subject line
  - Full email body
  - Strategic purpose (why this email exists in the sequence)
  - Tips for that specific email
- **Best Practices** — AI-generated advice for the sequence type

## Working with Results

- **Expand/collapse** individual emails to focus on one at a time
- **Copy** any email's subject or body individually
- **Download** the full sequence
- **Regenerate** with the same inputs for different variations
- The first email is expanded by default

## Tips for Better Sequences

- **Be specific about your audience** — "B2B marketing directors at mid-size tech companies" produces more targeted copy than "marketers"
- **Describe real pain points** — The AI weaves these into the email narratives
- **Set a clear CTA** — This shapes the final emails in the sequence
- **Match tone to relationship** — Use formal for cold outreach to enterprises, friendly for existing customers
- **Start with the default email count** — Each sequence type has a recommended count based on best practices
- **Edit after generation** — Use the generated copy as a starting point and customize for your brand voice
