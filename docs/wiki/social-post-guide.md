# Social Post Generator

Generate social media posts tailored to each platform's character limits, audience expectations, and content style. Create multiple variations across platforms in one go.

## How It Works

1. Enter your **topic** — what you want to post about
2. Select one or more **platforms**
3. Choose a **post type** and **tone**
4. Set the number of **variations** per platform
5. Toggle **hashtags** and **emojis** on or off
6. Click **Generate** to create your posts
7. Filter by platform, copy individual posts, or export all

## Platforms

| Platform | Character Limit | Optimal Length |
|----------|----------------|----------------|
| **LinkedIn** | 3,000 | ~200 characters |
| **Twitter/X** | 280 | ~200 characters |
| **Instagram** | 2,200 | ~150 characters |
| **TikTok** | 2,200 | ~150 characters |

Select multiple platforms to generate cross-platform content in one click. Each post is independently optimized for its platform.

## Post Types

| Type | Structure |
|------|-----------|
| **Thought Leadership** | Insights, opinions, industry perspectives |
| **Educational** | Tips, how-tos, lessons learned |
| **Promotional** | Product launches, features, offers |
| **Entertainment** | Humor, relatable content, memes |
| **Question** | Engagement-driving questions, polls, discussions |
| **Storytelling** | Personal stories, customer stories, behind-the-scenes |

## Features

### Hashtags

When enabled, the AI generates platform-optimized hashtags:
- **LinkedIn** — 3-5 professional/industry hashtags
- **Twitter** — 1-3 trending or niche hashtags
- **Instagram** — 5-10 discovery hashtags
- **TikTok** — 3-5 trending hashtags

Hashtags are shown as individual tags that can be copied separately.

### Emojis

When enabled, emojis are incorporated naturally into the post content. The style varies by platform — more playful for TikTok, more restrained for LinkedIn.

### Content Repurposing

Click "Repurpose existing content" to paste a blog post, article, or any long-form content. The AI will transform it into platform-appropriate social posts while preserving the key messages.

## Tips for Better Posts

- **One topic per generation** — Focused topics produce more coherent posts
- **Include key points** — Specific data points or examples make posts more engaging
- **Set a CTA** — Drives engagement (comment, share, click link)
- **Define your audience** — "SaaS founders" produces different content than "general public"
- **Generate 3-5 variations** — Pick the best or schedule them across different days
- **Repurpose long content** — Turn a blog post into a week's worth of social content

## Export Options

- **CSV** — Import into social media scheduling tools
- **JSON** — Structured data for automation
- **Copy All** — Full text of all posts to clipboard
- **Copy Individual** — Copy a single post with one click
